# Join

If you are excited to probe cellular responses with a mixture of experimental and computational techniques, come work with us! 

Want to do a [PhD on how fungi manage stress](https://www.findaphd.com/search/ProjectDetails.aspx?PJID=90405&LID=3300)? Apply now for this PhD project, working with us and also with [Atlanta Cook](http://cook.bio.ed.ac.uk/).

We will shortly (early 2018) be advertising for a Postdoctoral Research Assistant. We also welcome enquiries about postdoctoral scholarships, and visiting scientists including ERASMUS.
